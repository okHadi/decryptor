Decryptor (patent and trademark pending) is a free program
made for users so that they can invest into crypto more wisely.

The purpose is to save the people from "crypto gurus", and to create
more trust and trasperency between the common man and the big guns
of the industry so that more people invest in crypto without
being scammed.
